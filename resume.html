Welcome to my portfolio! This page is an accumulation of my learnings and skills in my journey to become a Front-end developer. This page will change from time to time as I learn new features. Once I am confident in my HTML and CSS knowledge, I will begin learning Javascript to make this site more responsive and user friendly!

I am a recent graduate from The University of Toledo in Toledo, Ohio. I graduated with a Bachelor's of Business Administration in Information Systems and a minor in Management and Information Security. One of my top classes throughout my four years at UT was my Intro to Web Development class. It mainly focused on HTML and CSS and the last week was a little intro to Javascript. While I didn't know it then, I know now that I want to become a full-time Web Developer!

My first goal is to become proficient enough in HTML and CSS that I can replicate almost any website I visit on my own. From there, I want to be able to fully immerse myself in the Javascript language. Front-end Developer is the position I want to hold first. Eventually, I want to learn back-end as well and become a Full Stack Developer!
